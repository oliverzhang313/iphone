IPhone 7 has six colors: gold, silver, rose gold, black, bright black and red. The home key has a new design, adding vibration feedback. It also supports IP67 anti splash, anti water and anti dust function, front / rear single camera, anti shake function, and adds a faster A10 fusion processor. The iPhone 7 cancels the 3.5mm headphone connector and introduces a new headphone, apple airpods, with W1 chip, sensor and battery, all in a small headphone body. The new iPhone has eliminated the 16GB model, with a minimum storage capacity of 32GB. The other two capacities are 128GB and 256gb.
